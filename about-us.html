About us

Bumi Restu Agri Nutrient Founded in 2008 and directly involved in the farming industry in malaysia, especially cattle and goats. We supply almost all products and services related to this industry. Most of our customers are comprise private sector breeders who breed cattle (beef / dairy) or goat (meat / milk).

Our main products are sourced from animal nutrition. We provide all types of drugs, vitamins and minerals related to the health of all animals in particular, Among our products are Vitamin AB, Anti-inflammatory, Iron , Anti-allergic, B-complex, Calcium powder and others.

In addition, Our company also provides equipment and machinery related to the farming industry. For example, Chopper, harvester, Disposal Items and many more, Semen (Sperm) is also available in our supply list including gender certainty semen (Semen Sexen).

For your information, our company has Authorised Dealership of two international companies. Shoof international (New Zealand) and Nasco Ft.Atkinson (United States) who supply us with high quality products.

Founder of this company Mr. Siva is an individual who is very capable in this farming industry. He has formal training in New Zealand Farming in the country that is advanced in this industry, particularly in the ITO (Industrial Training Organization,NZ). His training in Artificial Insemination of course (AI) on the LIC (Livestock Improvement Corporation, NZ) also Made him a highly effective AI Technician.

The company also provides other service such a consulting services to design, shape and setting up farms, formula feeding, planting and care of grass and other aspects related to animal husbandry. At present, our company is in the process of launching a project visit to New Zealand farming for the first time.
